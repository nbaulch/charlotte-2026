NoDa (North Davidson) is Charlotte's designated arts district ‚Äî murals, galleries, music venues, breweries, and indie restaurants clustered around 36th Street and North Davidson Street. The LYNX Blue Line light rail runs through NoDa, connecting it to Uptown and South End. It's a genuinely interesting and energetic neighborhood for what it is.

What it is: a young singles and arts neighborhood. Median age: 33.3. Approximately 54% of residents are single and never-married. 71% of households are non-family households. This isn't editorializing ‚Äî these are the demographics.

Wrong demographic fit: This family's top priority is neighbors with young kids. NoDa has among the lowest concentrations of families with children of any neighborhood on this list. Finding neighbors with 4-year-old twins is not the NoDa experience ‚Äî it's genuinely rare. This single factor is the primary reason NoDa is a Skip.
